What We Do

SkillsUSA is a nationwide competition that works with educators and students to bridge the skills gap in the United States. Schools compete in various skills-based activities to prove that their school is the best in class.

Student Experience

Students learn vital technical skills that excel them into the modern-day workforce. Teachers and educators work with students to find their hidden skills and their 'golden ticket' into today's workforce, through various skills-based activites and partnering with job shadowing and internships.

Doors to Success

When one door closes, another door opens. SkillsUSA helps open doors to the real world for a student to thrive. SkillsUSA has a vast amount of competitions to appeal to any category and skill-based activity. Students will gain positive leadership skills and good team working abilities to survive in today’s business industry.
